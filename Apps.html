Everyday Use Applications

Canvas

Canvas is our LMS in which students use to get their class documents/assignments that you post.

Canvas Support

Notability

Notability is used on a daily basis for our students to take notes or write/draw on pdfs.

Apple Classroom

Apple Classroom allows you to watch what is happening on your student's iPad. You also can send them documents or lock them into certain apps.

Eduphoria

Eduphoria is used for testing, OCT tracking, planning and observations.

Classlink is an apps that has access to other apps for our students such as Eduphoria or Discovery Education.
